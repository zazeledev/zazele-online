Our Legacy

About Zazele Training & Development

Established in 2011, Zazele is a 100% woman and black-owned B-BBEE company. We specialize in educating individuals to set up, manage, and grow their own computer literacy hub businesses.

Our trainers are experts who bring real business experience and vast computer knowledge to every session, ensuring a high level of learning and motivation.
